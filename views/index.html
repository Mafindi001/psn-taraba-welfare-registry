About This Platform

The PSN Taraba State Welfare Digital Registry is an official platform developed to strengthen the welfare system of the Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria (PSN) Taraba State Chapter.

This system enables all registered pharmacists in Taraba State to:

  • Self-register and maintain their welfare profile
  • Add important dates (birthdays, anniversaries, etc.)
  • Receive automated celebration reminders
  • Update personal and next-of-kin information
  • Stay connected with the PSN welfare department

Data Protection: All data collected is handled in compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) and is used solely for PSN welfare purposes.
